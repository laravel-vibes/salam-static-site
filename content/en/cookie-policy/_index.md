**Effective:** 27 March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Dr Salam Jibrel Medical Center uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. Non-essential cookies are **disabled by default** and only activated with your explicit consent. This policy should be read alongside our [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy-policy/).

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. We also use tracking pixels, web beacons, and local storage.

- **First-party cookies** — set by our website, only readable by us
- **Third-party cookies** — set by external services (e.g. Google Analytics)
- **Session cookies** — deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies** — remain for a set period or until deleted

## 2. Why We Use Cookies

- Ensure website functions correctly and securely
- Remember cookie consent preferences
- Understand how visitors navigate our website
- Remember language and display preferences
- Measure marketing campaign effectiveness

We **never** use cookies to collect sensitive medical or health information.

## 3. Legal Basis

- **Exempt (no consent required):** Strictly necessary cookies for website function/security
- **Explicit consent:** All non-essential cookies (analytics, functional, marketing)

No pre-ticked boxes or deceptive design. Declining optional cookies never blocks website access.

## 4. Cookie Categories

### Strictly Necessary (Always Active)
Essential for core website functions: page navigation, form submission, security (CSRF), and consent preferences.

### Analytics & Performance (Requires Consent)
Collect anonymous usage data (popular pages, visit duration, errors). All data is aggregated and does not identify individuals.

### Functional (Requires Consent)
Remember your choices such as language, region, and booking progress.

### Marketing & Targeting (Requires Consent)
Track browsing activity for relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness. Only activated with explicit consent.

## 5. Key Cookies We Use

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|
| cookie_consent | Our Website | Stores consent preferences | 1 year |
| XSRF-TOKEN | Our Website | CSRF protection | Session |
| session_id | Our Website | Session state | Session |
| _ga | Google Analytics | Anonymous visitor stats | 2 years |
| _gid | Google Analytics | Daily pageview tracking | 24 hours |
| locale | Our Website | Language preference | 1 year |
| _fbp | Meta (Facebook) | Ad targeting | 3 months |
| YSC | YouTube | Embedded video stats | Session |

## 6. Managing Your Preferences

### Cookie Consent Panel
Use the cookie banner on first visit, or click "Cookie Settings" in the footer.

### Browser Settings
Manage, block, or delete cookies through your browser settings.

### Third-Party Opt-Out Tools
- Google Analytics: Opt-Out Browser Add-on
- Facebook/Meta: facebook.com/ads/preferences

**Note:** Blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent booking forms and contact forms from working.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We review this policy at least annually. Material changes will display a prominent website notice and may re-request consent.
