{{% blocks/feature title="IVF (In Vitro Fertilization)" icon="fa-solid fa-flask" %}}

{{% blocks/feature title="ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)" icon="fa-solid fa-microscope" %}}
Advanced micromanipulation technique where a single sperm is injected
directly into an egg, ideal for male factor infertility.
{{% /blocks/feature %}}

{{% blocks/feature title="IUI (Intrauterine Insemination)" icon="fa-solid fa-syringe" %}}
A less invasive fertility treatment that places prepared sperm directly
into the uterus during ovulation.
{{% /blocks/feature %}}
