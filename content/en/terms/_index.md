## 1. Who We Are

Dr Salam Jibrel Medical Center is a privately licensed medical centre in Bahrain specialising in obstetrics, gynaecology, fertility, and women's health, regulated by the NHRA under Law No. 21 of 2015.

## 2. Scope of Services

**Obstetrics & Maternity:** Antenatal consultations, pregnancy monitoring, ultrasound scans, high-risk pregnancy management, postnatal care.

**Gynaecology:** Consultations, investigations, colposcopy, hysteroscopy, menopause, endometriosis, PCOS management.

**Fertility & Reproductive Medicine:** Fertility assessments, IVF, ICSI, IUI, egg/sperm/embryo freezing and storage, fertility preservation.

Service availability is subject to clinical capabilities, staffing, and regulatory approvals.
